XSLT Training

Radu Coravu
17 Jun 2021
Putting together a list of resources (courses, books, training) valuable for learning XSLT:
  • Michael Kay's "XSLT 2.0 and XPath 2.0", especially Part I: Foundations.
  • XSLT Frequently Asked Questions maintained by Dave Pauson.
  • XSLT training courses by Liam Quinn.
  • XSLT training by Tomos Hillman.
  • An Udemy course by Ken Holman containing also some free lectures.
  • XSLT Training courses by Dimitre Novatchev on PluralSight.
  • XSLT consulting by Mulberry Technologies.
  • Our Oxygen XML training partners.
